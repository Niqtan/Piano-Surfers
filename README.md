# yippee-ano
[zine]
## What it is
Inspired by the many falling-light youtube piano covers and games such as Piano Tiles, we decided to make a piano game that combines the two with hardware. Fish-themed, after the player hits certain notes, they will be propelled through the water to reach greater distances.
